Diamond no Ace

ANIME
★★★★★

As someone who himself played baseball, this anime hits all the right notes. The character development, intense matches, and emotional highs and lows are portrayed brilliantly. It captures the spirit of teamwork and perseverance like no other sports anime.

// MUST WATCH // SPORTS // LONG SERIES

Dispatch

GAME
★★★★☆

Dispatch is truly a hidden gem in the indie game scene. Its unique blend of narrative-driven gameplay and strategic decision-making creates an immersive experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat. The art style is charming, and the story is both engaging and thought-provoking. Audio wise it can use some improvement and the hacking elements are a hastle at best.

// HIDDEN GEM // NARRATIVE // INDIE

If you enjoyed We are the Police or the Boys this might be for you!
